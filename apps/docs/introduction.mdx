## Types of Functions Available

Atomic Toolkit provides a variety of functions to help you build your assets. Here are some of the most common ones:

-   **Atomic Asset Functions**: Functions related to creating and fetching atomic assets.
-   **Collection Functions**: Functions related to creating and fetching collections.
-   **GraphQL Functions**: Functions related to fetching data from the Arweave GraphQL API.

## Future Plans

We're always working on improving Atomic Toolkit. Here are some of the things we're working on:

-   **Stamp Functions**: Functions related to creating and fetching stamps.
-   **Atomic Tweet Functions**: Functions related to creating and fetching atomic tweets.
-   **UCM Marketplace Functions**: Functions related to creating and fetching marketplace listings.
-   **UDL Functions**: Functions related to verifying and fetching Universal Data License (UDL) data.
